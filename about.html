Our Mission

At our company, we believe in responsible tourism that respects local communities, cultures, and environments. We are committed to sustainable development and conservation, and work closely with local organizations and initiatives to ensure that our trips have a positive impact on the places we visit. We believe that travel is not only a way to explore new places, but also a way to connect with people and learn about different ways of life. We are dedicated to providing authentic travel experiences that immerse our clients in the local culture and memories for a lifetime.

Meet Your Dream Team

Emmanuel Baptister, founder of Bapta Safaris, is passionate about wildlife conservation. Growing up in Tanzania, he started his own safari business to share his love for the beauty and biodiversity of Tanzania while supporting local communities. Your safari dream team has many years of safari guide experience serving customers around the globe. At Bapta, you're more than a customer - you're our "rafiki", and there are no problems, only solutions. Hakuna matata!
